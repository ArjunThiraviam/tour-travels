Destinations

Rameswaram

Rameswaram is in the southeast Indian state of Tamil Nadu. It’s known for Ramanathaswamy Temple, a Hindu pilgrimage site with ornate corridors, huge sculpted pillars and sacred water tanks. Devotees bathe in the waters of Agni Theertham, off the beach east of the temple. Gandamadana Parvatham is a hill with island views.

Kanyakumari

Kanyakumari is a coastal town and it was known as Cape Comorin during British rule and is popular for watching sunrise and sunset over the ocean. The main sight seeing places are Thiruvalluvar Statue, Vivekananda Rock Memorial, Gandhi Memorial Mandapam, Tsunami Memorial Park, Bhagavathy Amman Temple and Kamarajar Mani Mantapa Monument.

Kodaikanal

Kodaikanal is a wonderful tourism place in South India. Our package covers the travel and visit to the fabulous places like Bryant Park, Coackers’walk, Pillar rock, Suicide Point, Silver Falls, Lake, Bear – Shola Falls, Kurinji-Andavar temple.

Thekkady

Thekkady is a beautiful tourism place located in the state of Kerala. Kerala state is known as "God's Own Country" because of its beautiful nature. Top places to visit in Thekkady are Periyar National Park, Periyar Lake, Murikkady, Permade, Pandikuzhi, Chellarkovil, Gavi, Managaladevi Temple and Ramakkalmedu.

Ooty

Ooty is a Beautiful Hill Station which is also called Ootacamund, Udagamandalam and Udhagai. The most popular visiting places in Ooty are Botanical Garden, Lake & Boating, Pine Forest, Nilgiris Mountain Rail, Deer Park, Doddabetta Peak, Ooty Rose Garden, Ooty Toy Train, Ooty Stone House, Toda Huts and more.

Munnar

Munnar is an exotic hill station with enclosed calm of serenity and beauty is the most visited destination in South India. The places to visit are Eravikulam National Park, Munnar Tea Museum, Pothanmedu View Point, Mattupetty, Attukal Waterfalls, Nyayamakad and Devikulam.

Courtallam

Courtallam or Kuttalam, is at a mean elevation of 160 m on the Western Ghats in Tirunelveli District of Tamil Nadu. There are many falls namely Main Falls (Peraruvi), Small Falls (Chittaruvi), Shenbaga (Chembakadevi) Falls, Honey Falls (Thenaruvi), Tiger Falls (Puli Aruvi), Five Falls (Aintharuvi) and Old Falls (Pazhaya Courtallam).

Thanjavur

Brihadishvara Temple, also called Rajarajesvaram or Peruvudaiyār Kōvil, is a Hindu temple dedicated to Shiva located in Thanjavur, Tamil Nadu, India. It is one of the largest South Indian temples and an exemplary example of a fully realized Dravidian architecture.

Palani

Palani is home to one of the most sacred shrines of the Lord Muruga, as worshiped in the Hindu sect of Kaumaram. The Thandayudhapani Temple dedicated to Lord Murugan "Palani Andavar", and regarded one of his Arupadai Veedu (Six Battle Camps), is situated here. The temple is situated atop a hill known as Sivagiri.

Thiruchendur

Tiruchendur is a panchayat town located at the Coromandel Coast off the Bay of Bengal, in the Thoothukudi district of Tamil Nadu. It is home to Thiruchendur Murugan Temple, also known as Arulmigu Subramaniaswamy Temple which is a Second Arupadai veedu.

Velankanni

Velankanni has one of the country's biggest Catholic pilgrimage centres, the Basilica of Our Lady of Good Health. Devoted to Our Lady of Good Health, it is popularly known as the "Lourdes of the East". Its origins can be traced back to the 16th century.

Allapey

It is an important tourist destination in India. The Backwaters of Alappuzha are the most popular tourist attraction in Kerala. A houseboat cruise in these backwaters can be booked. It connects Kumarakom and Cochin to the North and Quilon to the South. Apart from houseboat services, the Kerala State Water Transport Department provides government boat services within the district.
